Frequently Asked Questions

Why does Morning Mindful need Accessibility permissions?

The Accessibility Service allows the app to detect when you open a blocked app (like Instagram) and redirect you to the journal instead. It does NOT read content from other apps or track your activity - it only sees which app is in the foreground.

What's the difference between Full Block and Gentle Reminder?

Full Block: Instantly redirects you to the journal when you open a blocked app. Requires Accessibility permission.
Gentle Reminder: Shows a dismissible reminder overlay. You can choose to continue or go to your journal. Requires Usage Access permission.

Is my journal data private?

Yes! All your journal entries are stored locally on your device with AES-256 encryption. We never see, collect, or transmit your entries. Your data belongs to you.

What happens if I uninstall the app?

Your journal entries will be deleted. We strongly recommend creating an encrypted backup first (Settings ‚Üí Data ‚Üí Export Journals).

Can I use Morning Mindful in Chinese?

Yes! The app fully supports Chinese (Simplified). It will automatically use Chinese if your phone's language is set to Chinese. Chinese characters are also properly counted (each character = 1 word).

Why isn't the app blocking working?

Make sure you've granted the required permissions (Accessibility or Usage Access). On some phones, you may also need to disable battery optimization for Morning Mindful in Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí Morning Mindful ‚Üí Battery.
