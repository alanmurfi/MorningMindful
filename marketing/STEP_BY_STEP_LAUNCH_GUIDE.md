## REALISTIC EXPECTATIONS

**Month 1 Goals:**
- 500+ downloads
- 4.5+ star rating
- 20+ reviews
- 100+ social followers

**What success looks like:**
- Steady organic downloads (not spiky)
- Users coming back (DAU/MAU ratio)
- Positive review sentiment
- Word of mouth mentions

**Common pitfalls to avoid:**
- ❌ Buying fake reviews (will get you banned)
- ❌ Spamming subreddits (will get you banned)
- ❌ Ignoring negative feedback (hurts ranking)
- ❌ Stopping promotion after launch week (momentum matters)
