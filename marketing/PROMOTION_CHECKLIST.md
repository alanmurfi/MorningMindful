## Quick Reference: What to Do Each Day

**Every Day:**
- Check Play Console for reviews, respond within 24 hours
- Check social mentions
- 15 mins engaging in relevant communities

**Every Week:**
- One piece of content (post, comment, or article)
- Review metrics
- Plan next week's content

**Every Month:**
- App update (even small improvements)
- Evaluate what's working
- Set next month's goals
