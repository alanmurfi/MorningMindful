## One-Liner
Morning Mindful blocks social media apps until you write a short journal entry, helping you start each day with intention instead of distraction.

---

## Short Description (100 words)
Morning Mindful is a digital wellbeing app that addresses the modern problem of doom-scrolling first thing in the morning. Instead of fighting willpower, it creates a simple system: social media apps are blocked until you write a brief journal entry (default 50 words). The app features military-grade encryption for all journal entries, with all data stored locally on the device. Users can track their journaling streak, attach photos, and choose between "Full Block" or "Gentle Reminder" modes. It's designed for anyone who wants to reclaim their mornings and build a consistent journaling habit.

---

## Long Description (300 words)

Research shows that 80% of smartphone users check their device within 15 minutes of waking up, with social media being the most common first destination. This habit of "doom-scrolling" before getting out of bed has been linked to increased anxiety, reduced productivity, and difficulty focusing throughout the day.

Morning Mindful offers a different approach to digital wellness—one that doesn't rely on willpower. Instead of asking users to simply "use their phone less," the app creates a beneficial friction: when you try to open a blocked app during your morning hours, you're redirected to write a short journal entry first.

The concept is simple but effective. Users set their "morning window" (for example, 5 AM to 10 AM), choose which apps to block, and specify how many words they need to write to unlock them. The default is just 50 words—about 2-3 minutes of writing. Once completed, all apps unlock for the rest of the day.

Privacy is central to the app's design. All journal entries are encrypted using AES-256 encryption and stored exclusively on the user's device. There's no account creation, no cloud sync, and no way for anyone—including the developer—to access the user's private thoughts.

After several months of personal use, the developer has written more journal entries than in the previous five years combined—demonstrating how small, consistent friction can build powerful habits.

Morning Mindful is free to download with a small banner ad on the home screen. The journal writing experience itself is completely ad-free, ensuring users can focus on their thoughts without distraction.

## Key Features

- **Smart App Blocking** - Block any apps during configurable morning hours
- **Two Blocking Modes** - Full Block (instant redirect) or Gentle Reminder (dismissible overlay)
- **Encrypted Journal** - AES-256 encryption, all data stored locally
- **Photo Attachments** - Add images to journal entries
- **Timeline View** - Chronological view of entries with inline photos
- **Search** - Find past entries instantly
- **Streak Tracking** - Monitor consistency with daily streak counter
- **Auto-Backup** - Encrypted backups to user-chosen folder
- **Dark Mode** - System, light, or dark theme options
- **Daily Reminders** - Optional notification reminders
- **Multi-Language** - English, German, Chinese (Simplified)
- **No Account Required** - Works completely offline

## Company/Developer Background

Morning Mindful was created by Alan Murphy, an independent developer who built the app to solve his own morning doom-scrolling habit. After trying numerous digital wellness solutions that relied on willpower—which is in short supply at 6 AM—he designed an app that creates helpful friction instead.

The app has been in development since late 2025, with extensive personal testing before public release. It's built with modern Android development practices including Kotlin, Jetpack libraries, and a privacy-first architecture.
